Frequently Asked Questions

What is CineSeek?

CineSeek is your ultimate movie companion! Discover, explore, and organize films with ease using features like Search Movies to find any film in seconds. Trending Movies to stay updated on what’s hot, Watch on YouTube to stream trailers or movies instantly. Watchlist to save your must-watch picks. Whether you’re a casual viewer or a cinephile, CineSeek makes finding and enjoying movies simple and fun!

How do I search for movies on the app?

Finding your favorite movie is easy! Use the Search Movies feature by typing a title, genre, actor, or director in the search bar. You’ll instantly see results with movie details, ratings, and more. Try keywords like “action” or “Tom Hanks” to explore!

What are Trending Movies, and how are they selected?

The Trending Movies section showcases the most popular films right now, based on what’s buzzing in the movie world. These are updated regularly to reflect current viewer favorites, box office hits, or viral sensations, so you’re always in the loop.

Can I watch full movies directly on the app?

With the Watch on YouTube feature, you can stream trailers, behind-the-scenes clips, or full movies available on YouTube with just one click. If a movie isn’t available on YouTube, we’ll provide links to where you can watch it on other platforms, if applicable.

How does the Watchlist work?

The Watchlist lets you save movies you want to watch later. Just tap the heart icon next to any movie to add it to your list. You can access your Watchlist anytime from your profile to plan your next movie night!

How do I know if a movie is available in my region?

Availability depends on the platform hosting the movie (e.g., YouTube). When you use the Watch on YouTube feature, you’ll be redirected to YouTube, where region-specific availability will be shown. We’re working to provide more region-based guidance in the future.
